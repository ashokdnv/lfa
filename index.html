Laya Beats of Art

Art is an ocean with no boundaries and the passion for it is like the uncontrollable waves that sweep us off our feet into the awesome world of art.

The joyful involvement in the process of learning art will itself bring the phenomenal results, rather than bothering too much about the end result.

Laya Beats of Art is set up solely for the awareness and promotion of all forms of art in the society to understand, appreciate and follow the rich experience of art.

Laya Beats of Art conducts courses that combine the creative intelligence of its instructors with their technique & experience into training and development of different forms of art.
