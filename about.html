About Us

About Us

Laya Beats of Art located near one of the prime locations of Hyderabad city, the organization, with a difference, engages in providing tutelage to art enthusiasts of all ages.

Laya Beats of Art besides producing skilled artists, also works to bring out the creative and aesthetic abilities present in everyone.

We are here to address the issues that the population is facing today in a highly disorganized art training industry. Laya Beats of Art is passionate to deliver a professional service in order to raise the quality bar in terms of punctuality, great course content and commitment.

A pool of talented and experienced instructors backed by an efficient administrative back-end form the core of our execution engine.

Mission & Vision

The mission is to make all forms of art available under one roof.

Message

Every individual should know at least one form of art to relieve him/her from the current Era stress.
